Graphic Design & Layout

University of Kansas School of Music


Serenade Magazine


Serenade Magazine is published annually to showcase news and accomplishments of KU School of Music students, faculty, and alumni. As the school's primary publication, the magazine is key resource for student recruitment, alumni relations, and development.


CHALLENGE: limited real estate combined with an abundance of stories left us with very little visual "breathing room", resulting in several spreads housing 3 or even 4 separate articles.


REWARD: an abundance of dynamic candid photos and stories submitted by students and staff lent freshness and enthusiasm to the project, so even crowded layouts had energy and optimism.

Sound the Chant

The KU lookbook is the primary recruitment publication for the school of music.


CHALLENGE: The brochure contains essential information for prospective students including cost and financial aid, audition dates, ensemble information, and application deadlines within a very limited page-count while also including the most dynamic, interesting, and enticing features of the school.


REWARD: Beautiful professional photos taken during both special performances and in daily classroom scenes showcased the school's broad appeal without the need for extraneous copy.
