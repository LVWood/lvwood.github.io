Branding & Site Design

Ignea Strata


THE BRIEF: design and build a fully responsive website for a new, independent music ensemble. The site needs to establish the group's personality and aesthetic and provide a space for marketing and promotional materials.


REWARDS: with beautiful photo assets and a sophisticated aesthetic, the site lent itself to a simple, clean design with plenty of room to develop over time.


CHALLENGES: wide-angle photos with the subjects spaced far apart was lovely for landscape designs, but required some creative solutions to present the images effectively for mobile.

The mobile version stacks the animated banner text.

The desktop about page features a wide-angle shot of the ensemble with parallax scrolling.

In the mobile version, parallax is disabled and the wide-angle image is cropped into two sections featuring closeups of the singers.
