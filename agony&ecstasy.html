Marketing Designs & Promotional Videos

Agony & Ecstasy - A program by Ignea Strata

THE BRIEF: Produce striking marketing designs and promotional videos for a classical music concert by an emerging and relatively unknown music ensemble


CHALLENGES: At the beginning of their career, the ensemble didn't have a great deal of name-recognition and their programming was extremely interesting, but featured music that would be unfamiliar to typical audiences.


REWARDS: Drawing on the recognizeable imagery of Bernini's sculpture of St. Teresa, the design heightens the sensuality of the sculpture through overexposed highlights and an overlay of reds and pinks. The program was to feature several works by women composers, and the promotional videos offered a good opportunity to highlight the uniqueness of the program.
